About us:

"For us, every smile at the end of an event is a success story."

-Jose Oved Buritica, Owner.

Founded in 2003, our journey began with a passion for creating unforgettable moments. Today, we proudly serve clients across all Colombian territory, turning visions into extraordinary events.

At Zona Torrida, we specialize in bringing unforgettable events to life. From corporate gatherings to weddings, concerts, and private celebrations, our dedicated team works closely with you to turn your vision into reality.

We provide end-to-end event planning services, including:

  • Structure & Setup: High-quality staging, tents, and custom-built installations for any type of venue.
  • Audio & Lighting: Professional sound systems and lighting designs that create the perfect atmosphere.
  • Catering: A wide selection of menu options tailored to your event's style and budget.
  • Entertainment: From live music and DJs to performers and interactive activities, we keep your guests engaged and delighted.
